OVERVIEW

This course is an exploration of the opportunities and challenges of crowdsourcing and human computation, which are emerging fields that examine how humans can help solve problems that computers cannot solve yet. We will explore ways in which techniques from machine learning, game theory, optimization, online behavioral social science, and human-computer interactions can be used to model and analyze crowd-powered systems such as crowdsourcing markets, prediction markets, and user-generated content platforms.
