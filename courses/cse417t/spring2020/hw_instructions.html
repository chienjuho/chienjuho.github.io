• How to submit
    • All written solutions must be submitted via Gradescope (it can be typed or handwritten and scanned in, or a combination)
      • If your scan your written homework, please check the resolution and make sure it is readable.
      • For each question, make sure you click the page(s) that contains your answer for that question.
    • If there are programming questions, we will create another submission link also on Gradescope. Please submit your codes to Gradescope as well.
